Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner In Property Guardianship

Local Guardian is a 360° property management and consultancy company built for Non-Resident Indians with property in Kerala. We are a subsidiary of the ABS Group, a trusted business house with a 30-year legacy — which means the team guarding your home is backed by three decades of credibility, local networks and financial strength.

Kerala's climate is harsh and its property laws are complex. When you live abroad, a locked-up "ghost house" quietly deteriorates and vacant plots invite encroachment. We stand in for you — monitoring, maintaining, monetising and legally protecting your property, so you never have to fly down to fix a problem.

Backed by the ABS Group

Three decades of business heritage stand behind every commitment we make — you are never dealing with a fly-by-night operator.

Legal Expertise Built In

From Power of Attorney and mutation to title verification and FEMA-compliant repatriation, our legal desk handles it end to end.

One Trusted Point of Contact

Maintenance, tenants, taxes, paperwork, airport pickups — one accountable team handles it all, transparently.
