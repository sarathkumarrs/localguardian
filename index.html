What We Do

A Complete Ecosystem For Your Property

Five pillars of service that cover every physical, financial and legal need of your Kerala property.

01

Asset Monitoring & Maintenance

The "Peace of Mind" package — because in Kerala's monsoon, physical care is the top priority.

  • Monthly / bi-monthly HD photo & video inspections
  • Monsoon-proofing & roof waterproofing
  • Garden & plot upkeep, encroachment checks
  • Electricity, water & land/building tax payments
02

Income Generation

Turn a locked-up "ghost house" into an earning asset without lifting a finger.

  • Vacation rental / Airbnb management
  • Verified long-term tenancy & rent collection
  • Legally sound lease agreements
  • Interior & staging to boost rental value
03

Real Estate Consultancy & Sales

An unbiased expert on the ground for when you decide to buy or sell.

  • Transaction facilitation & sales liaison
  • Honest, local market valuation
  • Title verification (EC, deeds, mutation)
  • Repatriation assistance (FEMA, 15CA/CB)
04

Legal & Documentation Support

The biggest pain point for overseas owners, handled end to end.

  • Power of Attorney (PoA) drafting & registration
  • Mutation (Pokkuvaravu) & land survey
  • Village Office coordination
  • TDS & income-tax filing for rental income
05

Arrival-to-Departure Concierge

A true "homecoming" experience for when the family visits Kerala.

  • Pre-arrival deep clean & "live-in ready" setup
  • Airport pickup, car & driver, temple tours
  • Grocery & fridge stocking
  • Health concierge for elderly parents
★

The X-Factor: Dark Site Management

Specialist protection for vacant plots — the most common fear among NRIs.

  • "No Trespassing" boards with our contact info
  • Regular boundary & encroachment checks
  • Prevention of illegal occupation
